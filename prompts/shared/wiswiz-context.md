# WisWiz Context

## About WisWiz

WisWiz is an educational platform focused on mathematics learning for Dutch students. We provide:
- Interactive math exercises
- Personalized learning paths
- Progress tracking for students and parents
- Support for various Dutch education levels (basisonderwijs, voortgezet onderwijs)

## Tone of Voice

- Friendly and approachable
- Encouraging and supportive
- Professional but not formal
- Educational without being condescending

## Common Topics

1. **Subscriptions**: Questions about pricing, plans, trials
2. **Technical Support**: Login issues, app problems
3. **Content**: Questions about exercises, curriculum coverage
4. **Progress**: How to track student progress
5. **Schools**: Partnerships with schools

## Key Information

- Website: wiswiz.nl
- Support hours: Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00
- Response time goal: Same day for urgent, 24h for normal

## Language

- Primary language: Dutch
- Also support: English
- Use formal "u" for parents/adults, informal "je/jij" for students
